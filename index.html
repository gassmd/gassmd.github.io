Who am I?

Clearly not a photoshop expert...

My name is Michael and I'm a Computer Science Student and aspiring Full-Stack Software Developer looking for their first opportunity in the technology and software industry. My self-start mentality makes me highly motivated and always ready to learn about the newest technologies. I'm craving an opportunity where I can utilize both the soft skills I've gained from my previous work experience in hospitality, and the knowledge I've gained from learning object-oriented programming and software development.

The majority of my experience deals with Java, yet I have knowledge of various other technologies such as HTML/CSS/Javascript, JavaFX, SQL and Python among others.

My background in service provides for great attention to detail on indivdual product, while also balancing multiple objectives and responsibilities during more fast-paced events. I value the experiences and skills I've gained in the service industry, as I see them as necessary for a career in software development. I've gained experience in working with a large team, establishing business and personal relationships with clientele, learning how to listen to their needs and qualifications, and creating a customized product that myself and the rest of my team knew they would enjoy. Additional time-spent in business accounting gave me experience with handling sensitive information, communicating with other business's, and adapting to changing environments to meet certain deadlines such as end of the month audits.

With the personal and social skils I've gained from my background in service, and the techinical knowledge I've gained from my self and educational learning, I'm waiting for the right opportunity to make an impact at an influential company. I'm always eager to learn and have bene craving that first chance to apply all my newfound skills and to meet new goals. Feel free to contact me!
